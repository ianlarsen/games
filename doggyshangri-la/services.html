Board & Train

Our Flagship Service

In our ten-day program your dog lives with us in a calm, structured home environment, receiving multiple one-on-one training sessions per day. We focus on building a strong foundation of obedience, manners, and confidence.

  • Leash, threshold, and food manners
  • Core commands (Sit, Down, Place, Come)
  • Calmness around distractions
  • Real-world socialization

Boarding

Boarding with us feels like a second home. Your dog enjoys free access to the home and fenced yard, supervised play, and plenty of cozy downtime. We maintain structure throughout the day so your pup stays balanced, relaxed, and happy while you’re away.

This is ideal for dogs who are social, comfortable in a home environment, and do not require intensive training.

Doggy Daycare

Open daily from 6:00 AM to 7:00 PM, our daycare provides a safe, home-like environment where dogs can play, learn, and relax while you’re away.
