About Nicole

My Story

My bond with dogs began in childhood with a German Shepherd always at my side. Sixteen years ago we adopted another Shepherd from a rescue, and our home quickly turned into a haven for fosters. In one busy year we cared for nearly forty rescues—each with its own story and set of challenges. Helping those dogs gain confidence and manners revealed my ability to understand and communicate with them. That experience planted the seed for what would become Doggy Shangri‑La.

Our Training Philosophy

Training should foster clarity, confidence, and calm. Because every dog learns differently, I customize my approach to suit their energy and needs. Our balanced training pairs structure with compassion: we set clear expectations while rewarding curiosity and effort. When dogs trust and understand us, they blossom. My mission is to build that bridge of understanding between you and your dog.
