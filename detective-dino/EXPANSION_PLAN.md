## üîç HOW DIFFERENT CULPRITS WORK FOR EACH CASE

### For Case 1 (Missing Cookies):
**If BUNNY is guilty:**
- Clue 1: Carrot-shaped prints near jar (ironic twist)
- Clue 2: Bunny fur on cookie jar
- Clue 3: Cookie crumbs in bunny's favorite spot
- Clue 4: Hidden cookies in the garden with carrot peels
- Dialogue: "I... I couldn't resist! They smelled like carrots!"

**If CAT is guilty:**
- Clue 1: Cat fur on cookie jar
- Clue 2: Paw prints leading to cat's bed
- Clue 3: Cookie crumbs on the couch
- Clue 4: Hidden cookies under a cushion
- Dialogue: "Okay fine! I wanted to try them!"

**If DOG is guilty:**
- Clue 1: Large muddy paw prints
- Clue 2: Cookie crumbs in the backyard
- Clue 3: Dog's collar has cookie smell
- Clue 4: Cookies buried in the garden
- Dialogue: "Woof... I buried them for later!"

**If MOUSE is guilty:** (CURRENT DEFAULT)
- Clue 1: Tiny paw prints
- Clue 2: Small footprints trail
- Clue 3: Cookie crumbs path
- Clue 4: Hidden cookies behind flowers
- Dialogue: "I'm sorry! I was so hungry!"

### For Case 2 (Broken Toy):
**Each suspect has unique:**
- Different clue types (paint chips, fabric pieces, tool marks)
- Different hiding spots for broken pieces
- Different motivations (accident, jealousy, playing too rough)
- Different confession dialogues

### For Case 3-5:
**Same pattern - each suspect can be guilty with unique clue sets**
